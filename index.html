NEUROLOGY

Neurology is the medical field that studies and treats disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Neurologists diagnose conditions like strokes, epilepsy, and Parkinson’s disease using tools like MRI, EEG, and nerve conduction studies. Treatment includes medications, lifestyle changes, rehabilitation, and, in some cases, surgery by neurosurgeons.

PULMONARY

Pulmonary relates to the lungs and their role in breathing. It involves oxygen intake and carbon dioxide removal. Conditions like asthma and COPD affect lung function. Maintaining lung health is essential for overall well-being.

CARDIOLOGY

Cardiology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the heart and blood vessels. It deals with conditions like heart disease, hypertension, and arrhythmias. Cardiologists diagnose, treat, and help prevent cardiovascular diseases. A healthy lifestyle and regular checkups are key to heart health.

DENTAL SURGERY

Dental surgery involves surgical procedures on the teeth, gums, and jaw. It includes tooth extractions, implants, and corrective jaw surgeries. These procedures help treat dental issues, improve oral health, and enhance function. Proper aftercare ensures faster healing and long-term success.

ORTHOLOGY

Orthology is the study of corresponding genes in different species that evolved from a common ancestor. These genes, called orthologs, often retain similar functions across species. Understanding orthology helps in evolutionary biology and medical research. It is crucial for identifying gene functions and developing treatments

DERMATOLOGY

Dermatology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the skin, hair, and nails. It deals with conditions like acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer. Dermatologists diagnose, treat, and help prevent skin disorders. Proper skincare and medical treatments help maintain healthy skin.
